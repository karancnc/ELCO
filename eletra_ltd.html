About Electra LTD.

Electra Ltd. stands as a cornerstone of Israel’s infrastructure and engineering landscape, driving transformative projects in construction, real estate, and energy. With a global presence spanning multiple continents, Electra has established itself as a leader in delivering large-scale, complex solutions that shape the way people live, work, and connect.

From national infrastructure projects to urban development and renewable energy initiatives, Electra’s impact is far-reaching. Guided by a steadfast commitment to sustainability, operational excellence, and community development, every project is a testament to the company’s dedication to improving lives and shaping a more connected, sustainable future.

Notable Projects

National Library of Israel

The National Library of Israel, formerly Jewish National and University Library, is the library dedicated to collecting the cultural treasures of Israel and of Jewish heritage. The library holds more than 5 million books, and is located in the Government complex near the Knesset.

Haifa-Nazareth Light Railway

Electra is a key player in the construction of the Haifa-Nazareth Light Railway, a transformative public transportation project designed to improve connectivity in northern Israel. The project involves the construction of tracks, stations, and advanced electromechanical systems. Once completed, it will serve thousands of daily commuters, reduce congestion, and promote sustainable urban mobility.

Consumer Products

Through strategic partnerships like Carrefour and a strong focus on innovation, Electra CP consistently delivers cutting-edge solutions that enhance everyday life for millions of consumers, while expanding its influence and maintaining leadership across various sectors.

Zvika Swimmer

CEO of Electra Consumer Products

Zvika Shwimmer (44, married +3) is the CEO of Electra Consumer Products, appointed in Oct 2018.
Before, Shwimmer served as the CEO of Delta Israel for seven years and led the launching of the Fix underwear chain, established the group’s trading site, and led the expansion of Delta’s sales in Israel.
Shwimmer was also CEO of the Laline chain, part of the Fox group, and has managed Fox Israel for several years.
He holds a B.A. in business administration from the Peres Center for Peace and Innovation and an MBA from Bar-Ilan University.
