We Are Elco.

Elco Ltd. is a leading Israeli investment company, recognized for its ownership of the Electra Super Brand. With deep roots in industry, ELCO’s diverse portfolio spans multiple sectors including construction, infrastructure, consumer electronics, telecommunications, entertainment, energy, and more.

In 2023, ELCO generated an impressive annual turnover of approximately NIS 18.8 billion (about USD 5.1 billion). The company is publicly traded on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange (TASE), with a market value of around NIS 2.8 billion (about USD 770 million) as of May 2024. ELCO operates globally, with significant activities in over 15 countries and employs more than 23,460 people.
