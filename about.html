The Elco Group

Elco Ltd. is a leading Israeli company, owner of the Electra Super Brand. Rooted in industry, Elco’s companies today touch many lives at a variety of junctions – construction, infrastructure, consumer electronics, telecommunications, entertainment and more.

Legacy of Leadership

Elco, founded in 1949 by Alexander Salkind, is a leading public Israeli company with a strong presence in key industries such as construction, infrastructure, air conditioning, electronics, energy, gas, and food. Over the decades, Elco has become a significant force both in Israel and internationally, operating in 20 countries and employing over 20,000 people. The group’s growth is driven by strategic acquisitions of leading companies, which align with Elco’s vision of creating synergy across its diverse sectors. Gershon Salkind, a visionary leader, played a pivotal role in expanding the company and making it a global powerhouse.

Today, his sons Daniel and Michael Salkind , who serve as Co-CEOs, continue to lead Elco, preserving the values and forward-thinking approach that their father instilled. Elco’s board ensures that the company operates according to its core values and strategic goals, maintaining its leadership position through the work of its subsidiaries: Electra, Electra Consumer Products, Electra Real Estate, and Supergas Energy.

Business Focus

Rooted, passionate, innovative, people driven & determined to win. Our values are part of our DNA. They guide the way we work with our business partners, within our communities and with each other.

Deeply rooted in industry, Elco has a long track record of excellence in operating within complex systems and businesses.
Core activity - acquiring controlling shares in leading companies within their fields.

Business focus - creation of expertise in defined operational segments. Intelligent and responsible growth and development of long-term businesses while disposing of assets that have exhausted their growth potential.

Daniel Salkind

Co-CEO, Director

Mr. Daniel Salkind has served as Co-Chief Executive Officer and Member of the Board of Directors of Elco Holdings Ltd since 2002.

Mr. Salkind's other jobs include Chairman of the Board of Directors of ECP, and he also serves as director at public and private companies.

Mr. Daniel Salkind received his Bachelors degree in Economics from Haifa University, and his MBA from Northwestern University, in cooperation with Tel-Aviv University.
